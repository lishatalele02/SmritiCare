FAQs

What is SmritiCare?

SmritiCare is a platform designed to help in the early detection and diagnosis of Alzheimer’s disease using deep learning models and MRI scans.

How does SmritiCare work?

SmritiCare uses advanced deep learning algorithms to analyze MRI scans. The platform predicts the likelihood of Alzheimer’s disease and provides stage classification for better understanding and intervention.

Is the diagnosis 100% accurate?

While our models are trained for high accuracy, no diagnosis system can guarantee 100% accuracy. Always consult with a healthcare professional for confirmation and further guidance.

How can I upload MRI scans?

You can upload MRI scans via the Diagnosis page. Simply fill the personal information and click on the "Choose MRI image" button, select and upload your MRI image, then click on "submit" button for analysis in diagnosis form.

Is SmritiCare free to use?

Yes, SmritiCare is free to use for diagnosis purposes.
