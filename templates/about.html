About Us

Our Mission

At SmritiCare, our mission is to provide accessible and accurate Alzheimer’s disease detection tools to individuals and healthcare professionals. We aim to utilize advanced deep learning models to aid early diagnosis, enabling timely intervention and better care for patients.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a future where Alzheimer’s disease is diagnosed at the earliest stage possible, reducing its impact on individuals and families. By combining innovation, data science, and medical expertise, SmritiCare aspires to revolutionize Alzheimer’s care globally.

How To Use

Follow these simple steps to use the Alzheimer's Disease Prediction System effectively.

Log In

First, log in or sign up for an account to get started with the system.

Diagnosis

Go to the diagnosis tab, fill in the required information, and upload an MRI scan image in .jpg or .png format.

Submit and Download

Click on submit, and download your detailed diagnosis report instantly.
